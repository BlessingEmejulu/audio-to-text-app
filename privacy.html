Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2025

Simple Summary:
  • Audio is sent to Google Cloud for transcription
  • Text is sent to Google Gemini for AI summaries
  • Your notes are saved locally on your device
  • We don't store your audio or transcriptions on our servers

What Forword Does

Forword converts your speech to text using Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API and creates AI summaries using Google's Gemini API. Your notes are saved locally in your browser.

Important: When you use Forword's transcription and summary features, your audio and text are temporarily processed by Google's services.

What We Collect

Data We Don't Store:

  • Your audio recordings (sent to Google but not stored by us)
  • Your transcription text (processed by Google but not stored by us)
  • Personal account information (no registration required)
  • Browsing history or cookies

Data We May Collect:

  • Feedback you voluntarily submit through forms
  • Basic error reports to fix bugs (no personal content)
  • Anonymous usage statistics

Third-Party Services

Forword uses these Google services:

  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text: Converts your audio to text
  • Google Gemini API: Generates AI summaries of your transcriptions

When you use these features:

  • Your audio is sent to Google Cloud for processing
  • Your transcribed text is sent to Gemini for summarization
  • Google processes this data according to their privacy policies
  • We don't store copies of your audio or text on our servers

Google's Privacy Policies

Your data processed by Google services is governed by:

  • Google Cloud Privacy Notice
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Speech-to-Text Data Usage

How Your Data is Protected

  • No Storage: We don't store your audio or transcriptions on our servers
  • Local Notes: Your saved notes stay in your browser's local storage
  • Secure Transmission: Data sent to Google uses encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • No Accounts: No login required, no passwords to manage
  • Temporary Processing: Audio and text are only processed temporarily by Google

Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt-Out: Don't use voice transcription or AI summary features if you prefer not to use Google services
  • Local Control: All saved notes are stored locally on your device
  • Delete Data: Clear your browser data to remove all local notes
  • Export Notes: Download your transcriptions anytime

Data Retention

  • Our Servers: We don't store your transcriptions or audio
  • Google Services: Google may temporarily store data for processing - see their policies
  • Your Device: Notes remain until you delete them from your browser
  • Feedback: Voluntarily submitted feedback kept for 2 years

Children's Privacy

Forword is not intended for children under 13. If you're under 13, please have a parent or guardian use the service. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Forword users worldwide should be aware that:

  • Data is processed by Google Cloud services
  • Google operates globally with data centers worldwide
  • Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence
  • Google complies with applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page if anything changes and note the date above. Major changes will be highlighted in the app. Continued use means you accept any updates.
